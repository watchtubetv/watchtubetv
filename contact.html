Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Can I share my account with family?

Yes! Our family plans allow up to 6 profiles and simultaneous streaming on multiple devices.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your account settings or by contacting our support team.

What devices are supported?

We support Smart TVs, smartphones, tablets, computers, gaming consoles, and streaming devices.
