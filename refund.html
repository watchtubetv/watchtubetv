Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Quick Summary

Watch Tube TV offers flexible cancellation with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime and continue using the service until the end of your current billing period. No refunds are provided for partial months, but you keep access until your subscription expires.

1. General Refund Policy

Watch Tube TV operates on a prepaid subscription model. When you purchase a subscription, you are paying for access to our streaming service for a specific billing period (monthly or annually). Our general policy is that we do not provide refunds for partial periods of service.

2. Free Trial Period

2.1 New Members

New members are eligible for a free trial period as advertised at the time of signup. During this trial period, you can cancel your subscription at any time without being charged.

2.2 Trial Cancellation

  • You can cancel your free trial at any time before it expires
  • If you cancel during the free trial, you will not be charged
  • You will continue to have access until the end of the trial period
  • If you do not cancel before the trial ends, you will be automatically charged for the selected subscription plan

3. Subscription Cancellation

3.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel your Watch Tube TV subscription at any time by:

  • Visiting your Account page on our website
  • Selecting "Cancel Membership"
  • Following the cancellation instructions
  • Contacting our customer support team

3.2 Effect of Cancellation

  • Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period
  • You will continue to have full access to the service until the subscription expires
  • No partial refunds will be provided for the unused portion of your subscription
  • You will not be charged for subsequent billing periods

4. Exceptional Circumstances

In certain exceptional circumstances, we may consider providing refunds on a case-by-case basis:

4.1 Technical Issues

  • Prolonged service outages affecting your ability to access content
  • Technical problems that prevent normal use of the service
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges

4.2 Billing Disputes

  • Unauthorized charges on your account
  • Incorrect billing amounts
  • Charges after proper cancellation

5. Refund Process

5.1 How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund under our exceptional circumstances policy:

  • Contact our customer support team immediately
  • Provide details about the issue and reason for the refund request
  • Include relevant documentation (screenshots, error messages, etc.)
  • Allow up to 7 business days for review and response

5.2 Refund Timeline

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method
  • It may take additional time for your bank or credit card company to process the refund

6. Gift Subscriptions

6.1 Gift Refunds

  • Gift subscriptions are generally non-refundable once activated
  • If a gift subscription has not been redeemed, it may be eligible for refund
  • Contact customer support within 30 days of purchase for gift refund requests

7. Third-Party Purchases

If you purchased your Watch Tube TV subscription through a third party (such as Apple App Store, Google Play, or your cable provider), you must contact them directly for any refund requests. Our refund policy only applies to direct purchases from Watch Tube TV.

8. Promotional Offers

  • Special promotional prices are subject to their own terms and conditions
  • Promotional subscriptions follow the same refund policy as regular subscriptions
  • If you received a promotional discount, any refund will be based on the amount actually paid

9. Content Availability

We do not provide refunds due to:

  • Changes in our content library
  • Removal of specific movies or TV shows
  • Geographic restrictions on content
  • Personal preferences regarding available content

10. Disputed Charges

If you have a disputed charge on your account:

  • Contact us before disputing the charge with your bank or credit card company
  • We will work with you to resolve the issue quickly
  • Provide us with the opportunity to address the concern before initiating a chargeback

11. Policy Changes

We may update this refund policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

12. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy, please contact us:

  • Email: billing@watchtubetv.com
  • Phone: 1-800-WATCH-TV
  • Live Chat: Available 24/7 on our website
  • Business Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 8 PM EST
